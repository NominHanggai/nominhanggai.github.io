  • MN: Монгол ардын дууны үгийн цуглуулга.

  • CN: 一些蒙古民歌的歌词。有一些歌词中可能包含一些错误（特别是由传统蒙文转换来的西里尔文歌词），仍待有时间之后修正。这个外部链接中有更多老歌，但多数不是民歌。

  • EN: This is a collection of Mongolian folksong lyrics. Please note that some traditional or Cyrillic words may contain inaccuracies, as I have not yet had the opportunity to correct them. Please refer to this link for additional Mongolian songs, although most of them are not folksongs.

Below are Mongolian folksongs in the book 《蒙古民歌集》(Mongolian folksongs). The book has 165 folksongs and the songs were collected in 1948, in Zuu Uda League (昭乌达盟) and Jusotu League (卓索图盟/召斯图盟) (two cities in eastern Inner Mongolia) where Horqin Mongols, Baarin Mongols, and Tumed Mongols live. Most of the editors of the book were from 鲁迅艺术学院 (Lu Xun Art Institute). The list below is under construction.
