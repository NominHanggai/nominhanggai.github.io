About

I am a theoretical chemist interested in quantum dynamics and electronic structures of molecules and solids. I can be contacted by email (hanggai.nomin AT gmail.com). The list of my publications is available on the Google Scholar page.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, biking, and ice skating. I also love collecting and listening to Mongolian folk songs. I believe in fostering an inclusive scientific community that values diversity across race, ethnicity, socioeconomic background, disability, gender identity and orientation. By embracing a wide range of perspectives and experiences, we can drive innovation, enhance creativity, and solve complex problems more effectively.
